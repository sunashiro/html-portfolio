The Best Movies According to Ryan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Chef

This is a great feel-good movie that I love to re-watch when I need a pick-me-up.

MCU Movies

I'm a comic book nerd and, for better or worse, the MCU has had a massive impact on cinema.

Conjuring Movies

I'm also a fan of horror movies and the Conjuring movies have done for horror movies what the MCU did for all of cinema.
